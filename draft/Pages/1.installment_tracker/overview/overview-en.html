Overview

GENERAL DESCRIPTION

The payment tracking is based on a daily monitoring model, designed to provide real-time visibility on the evolution of repayments by subsidiaries, branches, regions and portfolio managers (PMs). This system allows to identify, on a daily basis, the gaps between expected due dates and actual payments made, in order to strengthen operational reactivity, risk management and the effectiveness of collection actions.

TABS

The model is structured in several complementary tabs, each providing a specific level of detail.

Tab Purpose
GLP View of expected vs. paid global outstanding during the month.
Installments Monthly installment details: expected, paid, unpaid.
Benchmark Comparison of key rates by subsidiary (Entry rate, Payment to date, etc.).
Subsidiaries Daily monitoring of expected and unpaid installments by subsidiary.
Customer details Detailed table of unpaid installments at customer level.
BranchDesc Daily monitoring of delays at branch level.
Region Daily monitoring of delays at regional level.
CategoryDesc Daily monitoring of delays at product level.
Installment overdue ratio Summary heatmap of daily overdue rates by entity.
